Eye Bank

Our eye Bank was started in the year 2012. Located at the 3rd floor ‘G’ block of the hospital. 600 sq.ft. of ample space to carry out operations. Consists of 3 separate labs with UV lamps.​

24/7 Pharmacy

Pharmacy was established in the year 2004 with motto to provide highest quality of pharmaceutical care through best practices. There are 2 exclusive IP pharmacies – one that is open for 24 hours on Ground Floor for OP/IP & the other open for 8 hours a day in the first floor. Five counters are available in our pharmacy and one counter is prioritised for emergency and ICU’s. Apart from 4 pharmacies avilable inside the hospital premises, One additional pharmacy is at extension OPD and 3 in our Urban and Rural health Centres.​
